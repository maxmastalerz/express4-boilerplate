About

Express4-boilerplate is an express 4.x boilerplate designed with simplicity in mind. It is a fork of atufkas/yanexbo.

This boilerplate utilizes MustacheJS for templating and is configured for use with Mongoose.

Getting Started

Alter the HTML files in /views/ and /view/partials

Alter the CSS files in /public/stylesheets/

Alter your routes in express-routes.js and /routes/
